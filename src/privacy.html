Our Privacy Policy

Artlink values the privacy and security of our users' personal information. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy in compliance with applicable data protection and privacy laws. This privacy policy explains the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the circumstances under which we may disclose it. Contact us for any questions.

When do we collect information?

When you use our NFT marketplace, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Account information, such as your username and password.
  • Biographical information, such as your bio or profile information.
  • Financial information, such as your wallet address.
  • NFT transaction information, such as transaction history, NFT ownership, and NFT metadata.
We may collect this information when you register for an account on our platform, when you interact with our services, or when you provide information voluntarily.

How do we use your data?

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with our services
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To communicate with you about our services, including providing customer support
  • To improve services by leveraging user analytics
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Who else can see your data?

We may disclose your personal information to third parties in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To service providers who assist us in providing our NFT marketplace services, such as hosting providers, payment processors, and analytics providers.
  • To law enforcement or regulatory agencies in response to a legal request
  • To protect our rights, or safety, or those of our users.
We will never sell your data to third-parties or advertising platforms.

How secure is your data?

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We also require our service providers to implement similar measures.

Will we retain your data?

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our NFT marketplace services and to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. We may retain some information for a longer period if necessary to protect our legal interests.

Do we use 'cookies'?

Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the site's or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information. For instance, we use cookies to help us authorise users, so that your data is only visible to only people we trust. They are also used to help us understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity, which enables us to provide you with improved services. We also use cookies to help us compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.

What are your rights?

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. You may also have the right to object to or restrict certain types of processing of your personal information. Please contact us using the information below if you wish to exercise any of these rights.
